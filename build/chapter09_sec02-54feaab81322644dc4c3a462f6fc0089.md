```{admonition} Learning goals
:class: attention
* [ ] You can partition a closed interval $[a, b]$ into $n$ subintervals of
  equal width and identify the corresponding **sample points**.
* [ ] You can form and compute left, right, and midpoint **Riemann sums** for
  a given function and partition.
* [ ] You know that a Riemann sum approximates the area under a curve by a
  finite collection of rectangles, and that the approximation improves as $n$
  increases.
```
