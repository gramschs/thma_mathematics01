# Computing Definite Integrals

```{admonition} Learning goals
:class: attention
* [ ] You can evaluate definite integrals of elementary functions by finding
  an antiderivative and applying the **Fundamental Theorem of Calculus**.
* [ ] You can split a definite integral at a point of discontinuity or sign
  change using the additivity property from Section 9.4.
* [ ] You can verify a result by checking that the antiderivative is
  consistent with the geometric interpretation of the integral as signed area.
```
