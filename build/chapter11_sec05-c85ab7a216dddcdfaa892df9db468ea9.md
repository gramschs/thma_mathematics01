```{admonition} Learning goals
:class: attention
* [ ] You know the two types of **improper integral**: integrals over
  unbounded intervals and integrals of functions with a vertical asymptote
  inside or at the boundary of the interval of integration.
* [ ] You can evaluate an improper integral by replacing the infinite limit
  or the singular point with a finite parameter and taking the limit of the
  resulting proper integral.
* [ ] You can determine whether an improper integral **converges** or
  **diverges** and interpret the result in the context of the running example.
```
