# Algebraic Operations with Complex Numbers

```{admonition} Learning goals
:class: attention
* [ ] You can add and subtract complex numbers by combining real and imaginary
  parts separately.
* [ ] You can multiply two complex numbers in Cartesian form by expanding the
  product and applying $i^2 = -1$.
* [ ] You can divide two complex numbers by multiplying numerator and
  denominator by the **complex conjugate** of the denominator to obtain a
  result in the form $a + bi$.
```
