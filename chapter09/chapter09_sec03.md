```{admonition} Learning goals
:class: attention
* [ ] You know the definition of the **definite integral** $\int_a^b f(x)\,dx$
  as the limit of Riemann sums as the number of subintervals tends to infinity.
* [ ] You can identify the components of the notation $\int_a^b f(x)\,dx$:
  the integrand, the variable of integration, and the limits of integration.
* [ ] You can interpret the definite integral geometrically as the **signed
  area** between the graph of $f$ and the $x$-axis on $[a, b]$.
```
