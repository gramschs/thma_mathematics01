* [ ] You know the notation $\int f(x)\,dx = F(x) + C$ for the **indefinite

  the constant of integration.
* [ ] You can apply the **linearity** of the indefinite integral: the constant
  factor rule and the sum rule.
* [ ] You can build and use a reference table of standard indefinite integrals
