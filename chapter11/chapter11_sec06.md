```{admonition} Learning goals
:class: attention
* [ ] You can identify which integration technique is most appropriate for a
  given integrand: direct antiderivative, substitution, integration by parts,
  or partial fractions.
* [ ] You are familiar with a systematic decision procedure for classifying
  integrands and selecting a method before beginning the calculation.
* [ ] You can combine multiple techniques in sequence when no single method
  resolves the integral on its own.
```
