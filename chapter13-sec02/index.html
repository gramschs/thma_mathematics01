Learning goals

  • You can add and subtract complex numbers by combining real and imaginary parts separately.

  • You can multiply two complex numbers in Cartesian form by expanding the product and applying i2=−1i^2 = -1i2=−1.

  • You can divide two complex numbers by multiplying numerator and denominator by the complex conjugate of the denominator to obtain a result in the form a+bia + bia+bi.
