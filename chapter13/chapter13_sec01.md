```{admonition} Learning goals
:class: attention
* [ ] You know that the equation $x^2 + 1 = 0$ has no solution in the real
  numbers, and that this motivates the introduction of the **imaginary unit**
  $i$ defined by $i^2 = -1$.
* [ ] You know the definition of a **complex number** $z = a + bi$, where
  $a, b \in \mathbb{R}$ are called the **real part** and the **imaginary
  part** of $z$.
* [ ] You are familiar with the set $\mathbb{C}$ of all complex numbers and
  understand that $\mathbb{R}$ is contained in $\mathbb{C}$ as the special
  case $b = 0$.
```
